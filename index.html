About the park

Small, family-run, and right on the coast.

Sandy Bay is a small, family-run caravan park in Towyn, between Rhyl and Abergele on the North Wales coast. We keep the park open for eight months of the year, so there's plenty of time to make the most of a caravan here — a quick weekend away or a whole summer.

We understand pets are part of the family too, so dogs and other well-behaved companions are always welcome. On-site Wi-Fi means you're never fully cut off, even when you'd rather be.
